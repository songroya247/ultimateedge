Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026 · UltimateEdge.info

The short version: We collect only what we need to run this site. We never sell your data. We never share it with advertisers. You are always in control.

Who We Are

UltimateEdge.info is a digital tools and resources website owned and operated by Solomon. We provide free AI tools, educational calculators, trading resources, and digital products for content creators and modern thinkers.

If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at: ultimateedge.info@gmail.com

What Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

Information you give us directly:

  • Your name and email address when you sign up for our email list
  • Your email address when you purchase a product through our Selar store
  • Any messages you send us through our contact form

Information collected automatically:

  • Basic usage data such as which pages you visit and how long you stay (via Google Analytics, if enabled)
  • Your browser type and general location (country/region level only — we never track your exact location)
  • Referral source — how you found our website

Information we do NOT collect:

  • We do not collect payment information — all purchases are handled by Selar, which has its own privacy policy
  • We do not collect any data from our free tools — prompts you generate are not stored on our servers
  • We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes

How We Use Your Information

  • To send you the email content you signed up for — weekly prompt drops, AI tutorials, and tool updates
  • To fulfil digital product orders placed through our Selar store
  • To understand how visitors use our site so we can improve it
  • To respond to your messages and support requests
  • To send important updates about our products and services

Email Communications

When you subscribe to our email list, you consent to receive our newsletter and product updates. Every email we send includes an unsubscribe link. You can opt out at any time with one click — no questions asked.

We use Google Sheets to store subscriber information. We do not use third-party email marketing platforms that sell or share your data.

Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies. Specifically:

  • Google Analytics cookies to understand site traffic (you can opt out via your browser settings)
  • Session cookies that help the site function correctly — these expire when you close your browser
  • Local storage on your device to remember your prompt history within a session — this stays on your device only

We do not use advertising cookies or tracking pixels from social media platforms.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services which have their own privacy policies:

  • Selar — processes payments for our digital products. See Selar's Privacy Policy
  • Google Analytics — anonymous site analytics. See Google's Privacy Policy
  • GitHub Pages — hosts this website. See GitHub's Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic Claude API — powers our AI prompt generation tools. Prompts are processed but not stored by us. See Anthropic's Privacy Policy

Data Storage and Security

Your email address and name (when provided) are stored in a private Google Sheet accessible only to us. We take reasonable steps to protect this information including restricted access controls.

We retain your email data for as long as you remain subscribed. When you unsubscribe, your data is deleted from our list within 30 days.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data at any time
  • Withdraw consent for email communications at any time via the unsubscribe link
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, email us at ultimateedge.info@gmail.com and we will respond within 7 days.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy
